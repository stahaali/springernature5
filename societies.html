Support for societies

  • Our portfolio of competitive and prestigious journals is hosted on our world-leading content platforms, which enable billions of articles to be downloaded each year.
  • Our open access leadership enables us to address and solve challenges faced by the research community, guiding the sustainable path to OA, speeding up the transition to full OA, and helping to better address the problem of funding availability.
  • Our exceptional author experience, from our submission system and web platform through to our production systems and collections tools, ensures that our partners are supported with the tools and services they need to exceed the expectations of their members and authors.
  • Our commitment to helping global researchers achieve their desired community impact and connect their research with the policymakers who use it to make decisions that shape society around the world.
